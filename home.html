About Us

Here at Koehler Baking Company, we pride ourselves on capturing the best parts of homemade baked goods, and boxing them up for our customers to enjoy. We want you to get the most out of our product, so we don't cut corners. We use the best ingredients available, and we lovingly prepare everything we make. Whether you try our world famous Chocolate Chip Cookies, our delicious Pistachio Cake, or our sweet and savory Pumpkin Bread, you'll be absolutely astonished by the quality of our baked goods.

Fortunately, our original baker is still managing our inventory, but as more of a supervisor, allowing us to expand our company while still maintaining the high quality we're known for.

Don't settle for bottom-tier baked goods, when you can go to Koehler Baking Company and get treated like family!

Cookie Facts

Did You Know?

It is often claimed that the invention of chocolate chip cookies was an accident. But it is on record that Ruth Graves Wakefield, creator of the original chocolate chip cookie, stated that she had intended to make the cookies.

While working as a dietician, Ruth Graves Wakefield bought a tourist lodge and named it the Toll House Inn. She cooked all of the food at the Inn, and her restaurant quickly gained local fame.

The myth is that Ruth ran out of the usual chocolate she used, so she switched to Nestlé semi-sweet chocolate. The chocolate didn't melt the same way, resulting in an "accidental" new treat.

Intended or not, chocolate chip cookes, then known as Toll House Chocolate Crunch cookies, were a massive success, which earned Ruth a lifetime supply of Nestlé semi-sweet chocolate.
